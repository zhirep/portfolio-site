Slimy Ascent

A 2D platformer where you play as a slime.

Project Summary

Slimy Ascent is a 2D platformer where the player must control the slime and guide it through challenging levels to safety. The project was developed in Unity and the sprites used were drawn in Asesprite. This was a project done in around 8 weeks and was completed with 3 other people.

My Role

  • Designed and drawn the visuals of the game.
  • Implemented the logic behind the hazards in the game.
  • Implemented one of the core features which is the wall slide.

Technologies Used

  • C#
  • Unity
  • Asesprite
