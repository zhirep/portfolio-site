Crossy Roads 3D

A top down platformer where you play as a bug.

Project Summary

Crossy Roads 3D is a top down platformer where the goal is to cross as many roads as possible without getting hit by the moving traffic. The project was developed in WebGL and assets were built from an in-house engine. This was a project done in around 8 weeks and was completed with another person.

My Role

  • Designed the assets of the game.
  • Implemented the logic behind the shader lighting.
  • Implemented coin and scoring logic.
  • Worked on the SFX and UI.

Technologies Used

  • WebGL
